Planning an event can take from weeks to months, it really depends on the size and the details that the event involves. Cancellation
  • Cancellations providing more than 48 hours notice in relation to a scheduled arrival date are refundable.
  • Cancellations inside 48 hours of the scheduled arrival date will result in a fee equivalent to the full deposit or 1 nights accommodation.

Refund: Where a deposit or prepayment is to be refunded, an administration fee will be charged. Alternatively the total refund can be transferred to a future booking at a later date to be used within 12 months of the refund date. In this case no administration fee will apply.

All events must have a budget. It’s the glue that keeps everything together. It tells us what is affordable on a small or sometimes large scale, yet we tend to want more than we are allowed. Having a budget plan is the most effective way for all event managers to stay on top of their budget. Keeping track of your budget is not an easy task, but one that is an absolute must to keep your event calculated and stress-free.
